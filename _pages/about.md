I'm a Computer Scientist and Industrial Engineer with 5 years of experience in Artificial Intelligence. I'm currently working as a Research Assistant in the Web Engineering Laboratory at the Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST).

In particular, I'm working on the development of Machine Learning models that use Cognitive and Knowledge Engineering mechanisms to provide services to users.

In the past, I worked as a Data Scientist and Research Assistant at the Complex Systems of Engineering Institute, University of Chile.
